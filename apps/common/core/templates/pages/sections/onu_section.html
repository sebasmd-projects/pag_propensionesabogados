UN DESA - ONU

{% blocktrans trimmed %} The law firm Propensiones Internacional supports the Fundación Attlas Org, REGISTERED in the database of the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (UN DESA - ONU), within the framework of its project ‘Colombia Debt Relief: A New Beginning’. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} This initiative seeks to strengthen asset recovery and economic stability through legal and financial mechanisms, promoting international cooperation and sustainable development. Its purpose is to combat extreme poverty and economic vulnerability by providing legal tools for financial rehabilitation, access to decent opportunities, and the construction of a fair and sustainable social balance. {% endblocktrans %}
